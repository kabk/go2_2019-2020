## Music styles

### Techno

160 - 200 BPM, 4/4 time signature

### House

120 - 130 BPM, "four-on-the-floor" -> bass drum on every beat, soft and "feathered", felt.

### Gabber

150 - 190 BPM, distorted square wave bass

### Dubstep

130 BPM, Wobble bass
